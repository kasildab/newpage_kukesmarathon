Course Description

The Half Marathon course takes you through the beautiful landscapes surrounding Kukës. Starting in the city center, the route follows paved roads through picturesque villages, with stunning views of the Albanian Alps. The course is challenging but rewarding with a mix of flat sections and rolling hills.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to participate?

Participants must be at least 16 years old on race day to compete in the Half Marathon.

Is there a bag check available?

Yes, we provide a secure bag check service at the start/finish area. Please use the clear bag provided at packet pickup.

Are headphones allowed during the race?

For your safety and the safety of others, we recommend against using headphones. If you choose to wear them, please keep the volume low and remain aware of your surroundings.

What is the refund policy?

Entry fees are non-refundable and non-transferable. This policy applies to all participants regardless of circumstances.

Will there be pace groups?

Yes, we will have pace leaders for the following finish times: 1:45, 2:00, 2:15, 2:30, and 2:45.
